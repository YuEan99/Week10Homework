Covid-19 Statistic

Interactive plot showing relationship and trend between Comfirmed and Death cases across different continents.

The scatter plot shows a very strong positive correlation between Comfirmed and Death cases. As the number of Comfirmed cases increases, we are expected to see an increase in the number of Death cases.

Overall, the more population a country has, the more Comfirmed and Death cases it has due to the higher chance of transmission.
